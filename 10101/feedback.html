Feedback, assessment, past papers : MATH10101

See also Course materials and About the course.

Feedback from us to you

  • This course is supported by feedback supervisions. You can get feedback on your understanding from your MATH10101 supervisor. Students can also get feedback on their understanding directly from the lecturer, for example during the lecturer's office hour (details on the main course materials page).
  • We provide written feedback on how the students did in past exam papers.

Feedback from you to us

  • You may email me or see me at any point during the course to give me feedback. See my contact details on the main course materials page.
  • You will complete a Unit Evaluation Questionnaire in the last 2 weeks of the semester. See my response to last year's student evaluations.

How we assess you

The final exam in January will test to what extent you achieved the Intended Learning Outcomes of this course. The mark for your final exam will be out of 75 and will be added to the supervision mark (out of 10) and the week 6 test mark (out of 15) to give a mark out of 100 for the MATH10101 course unit.
