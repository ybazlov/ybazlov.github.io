Assessment and feedback

This page tells you what assessment tasks you will need to complete and when. It also tells you what kind of feedback you will be able to receive.

Assessment tasks - for credit

Feedback quizzes

I give students an opportunity to sit a short feedback quiz at the beginning of every tutorial. The quizzes are for credit. Individual feedback will be offered where possible.

Students can also get feedback on their understanding directly from the lecturer, for example by asking a question in the lecture, in the tutorial, during a quiz or in the break.

The final test

This will be a 45-minute paper sat on the last day of the course, worth 50% of the final mark for the course unit.

Feedback from YOU

You will be asked to fill in a Unit Evaluation Questionnaire (UEQ) towards the end of the course.
